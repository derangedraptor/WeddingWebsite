Thank You for Your Kindness

We are beyond grateful for your love and support as we prepare to start our life together. Any gifts truly mean the world to us and will be of great benefit as we settle into our new home. Thank you for making this special time even more meaningful — we can’t wait to celebrate with you!

We will also be celebrating the traditional Chinese culture of gifting red envelopes or “li xi / 紅包” (lucky money) to the bride and groom as well. For those who would like to follow the custom, please provide the li xi to the bride and groom the day-of the wedding in a red envelope to bless their wedding day and married life.

Thank you so much,
Cammy and James
